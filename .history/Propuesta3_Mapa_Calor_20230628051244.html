Efectividad Entre Tipos

Description:

The graph above represents a heatmap illustrating the effectiveness between different types of Pokémon. Each type is indicated by color, and the heatmap shows which types are more effective against each other.

Stat Definitions:

  • HP: Hit Points - Represents the Pokémon's health or vitality.
  • Attack: Determines the strength of the Pokémon's physical moves.
  • Defense: Measures the Pokémon's ability to withstand physical attacks.
  • Sp. Atk: Special Attack - Determines the power of the Pokémon's special moves.
  • Sp. Def: Special Defense - Measures the Pokémon's ability to withstand special attacks.
  • Speed: Represents the Pokémon's agility and determines its order of action in battles.

It is important to note that a Pokémon can have one or two types (Type 1 and/or Type 2). This means that their effectiveness will be considered for both types when analyzing the heatmap.
